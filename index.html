The Adventure to Taiwan and Yogyakarta!

What is EduTrip?

EduTrip is a program in the school of Junior High School of Santa Laurensia, where Grade 8 students go to choices of destinations, such as Yogyakarta of Indonesia, and a variety of cities in Taiwan such as Hualien and Kaohsiung. This website will particularly be centered around the perspective of the students that visited the city of Kaohsiung in Taiwan.

  • Kaohsiung
  • Keelung
